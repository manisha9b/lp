Who are we.

For far too long, Healthcare has been an ambiguous, intimidating transaction for consumers like you and us. It has been even more complex or worse still, irrelevant, in the context of Employee Healthcare. Most organizations rely on Group Health Insurance or “Feel-Good” Health Checkup activities that provide very little incentive to the Employee and very little tangible employee health data to the organization’s HR Person or decision maker .

We at EasyBuyHealth believe that Healthcare should be Simple, Affordable & easy to Implement. It should also provide you – the HR or Admin person within your Organization-wide ‘Health Index’ on an Online Dashboard.

Real, Employee-Health Index data that help you plan your Group Health Insurance better and focus on building productivity within your organization
Your Employees will feel like you REALLY CARE and get relevant Health Data about themselves that can help them plan theirs and their family’s lives, better.

So talk to us. We are building a NEW kind of Healthcare Ecosystem, using Technology & the participation of Service Providers & Consumers, to ensure that Healthcare is TRANSPARENT, SIMPLIFIED & AFFORDABLE.

EasyBuyHealth is now live across select companies in Asia, providing unique and relevant Healthcare Service Products, through its Integrated Employee Healthcare Packages.
